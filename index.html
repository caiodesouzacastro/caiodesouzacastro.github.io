Caio de
Souza Castro

I am a professor at the São Paulo School of Economics (FGV EESP) and a researcher at FGV CLEAR and FGV Cidades.

My research is in applied economics, with an emphasis on causal inference and impact evaluation of public policies. I work on projects spanning education, health, labor markets, international trade, and environmental economics, drawing primarily on Brazilian administrative data.

I hold a Ph.D. and M.Sc. in Economics from FGV EESP and a B.A. in Economics from the University of São Paulo (USP).
